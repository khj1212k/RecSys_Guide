# 04. SOTA & GenAI

The emergence of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI is shifting the paradigm of recommender systems from "Prediction" to "Generation".

## Major Trends

### 1. [LLM-based](./01_LLM_Based/README.md)

Leverages the world knowledge and reasoning capabilities of massive models like GPT and LLaMA for recommendation. (LLM4Rec, P5)

### 2. [Multimodal RS](./02_Multimodal_RS.md)

Combines various modalities such as images, video, and audio, not just text, to better understand items and provide recommendations.

### 3. [Generative RS](./03_Generative_RS.md)

The most innovative approach. Instead of retrieving from millions of items, it directly generates the ID of the next item, just like ChatGPT generates words.
